I work freelance as a stylist and set dresser for the big and the small screen.

Aside from work, I love hula hooping and practising hoop tricks, performing with ‘Londons coolest choir’ [Time Out Magazine] somevoices.co.uk, and being a frazzled mummy to my little man.
