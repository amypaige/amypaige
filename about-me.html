Biography

I work freelance as a set dresser for the big and small screen, and have a strong interest in events, especially weddings; helping to design, decorate and dress the space.

I love creating interesting and dynamic sets, working alongside the client to achieve stunning visuals that meet the brief. I enjoy meeting new people, sharing creative ideas and putting together inspiring projects.

When I'm not 'working', I love to hula hoop to cheesey pop tracks, practice hoop tricks and being a frazzled mum to a highly energetic toddler.
